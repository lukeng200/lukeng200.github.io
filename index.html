About

I am a MSc student in the Joint Master Degree in Water and Coastal Management at the University of Bologna. My research is focused on Statiscal Analysis of Coastal Drainage Data in relation to Climate Change and Extreme Events, advised by Prof. Marco Antonellini and Prof. B.M. Giambiastini. My research is supported by the European Union through the University of Bologna. My Bachelor of Science thesis was, "Impact of Limetsone Mining on Water Quality".
