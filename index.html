About

I am a graduate student holding a Masters Degree (MSc) in (LM-75 - Environmental and land sciences) Environmental Assessment and Managementwith a major in Water and Coastal Managementfrom the University of Bologna, Ravennna Campus, Italy.

My current research is focused on Statiscal Analysis of Coastal Drainage Data in relation to Climate Change and Extreme Events in the Low-Lying Coastal Watersheds, supervised by Marco Antonellini and Prof. B.M. Giambiastini with collaboration with the land reclamation consortium.

My programme is fully supported by the European Union through the University of Bologna.
